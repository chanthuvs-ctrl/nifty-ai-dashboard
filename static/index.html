Intraday Trading Checklist & Routines

Systematize your day. Save checklist status locally to maintain perfect execution habits.

Pre-Market Checklist (08:30 - 09:15)

Check Global indices & GIFT Nifty for overnight bias (+/- gaps)
Mark Yesterday's High (YHI), Low (YLO), and Close (YCL) on chart
Plot Session Volume Profile and identify POC, VAH, and VAL
Read India VIX (Establish regime volatility brackets)
Check PCR (Put-Call Ratio) on active option chain for sentiment

Execution Checklist (During Market)

Wait for the opening 15-minute range to establish before entry
Confirm entries with Footprint delta buying/selling imbalances
Validate setup proximity to VWAP or Anchored VWAP anchors
Set position size strictly within 1% risk allocation rule
Input hard stop-loss and limit targets in terminal immediately

Post-Market Review (15:35 - 16:30)

Log all trades in the Positions table with reasons and screenshots
Calculate realized net P&L and compare against daily limits
Tag mistakes (FOMO, Chasing, Held past stop, Rules followed)
Review AI-assisted trade suggestions against actual entries
Shut down trading engine and clear mental space for recovery
